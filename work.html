Work

After finishing my MSc, I joined a 3D data visualization startup, 3point Science, as the first hire. In March 2016, the company was acquired by Seequent (formerly ARANZ Geo), a New Zealand based company which is known for the Leapfrog Geo 3D modeling software. Our team pioneered Leapfrog View, Seequent's cloud solution.
