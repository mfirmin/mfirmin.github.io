About Me

Hi, I'm Michael. I moved to Vancouver from Colorado three years ago to pursue my MSc in Computer Science at the University of British Columbia. I am interested in all things related to computer graphics. Having completed my MSc, I am currently working at a startup company, 3point Science. 3point focuses on interactive science visualization, trying to bring complex scientific concepts to the web in an easily accessible, interactive form.

For my MSc, I worked under Michiel van de Panne on physics based character animation, creating a simple scripting language to control the motion of a 2D humanoid character.

I'm hoping to use this website to show off some of my projects that I have done both for courses and in my spare time.

On the side, I enjoy trail running, playing piano, reading, climbing, and baking.
