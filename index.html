About Me

Hi, I'm Michael. I'm an independent video game developer and computer graphics enthusiast based out of Toronto, Ontario. I am currently in the process of developing my first video game, a Tactical, Turned Based RPG inspired by video games like Final Fantasy Tactics, and board games such as Gloomhaven and Mage Knight. More information coming soon! I try to keep up on the latest in 3D Graphics through my side projects.

Previously, I worked as the Visualization Lead of the Calgary branch of a geological visualization company, Seequent Solutions, For my MSc, I worked under Michiel van de Panne on physics based character animation, creating a simple scripting language to control the motion of a 2D humanoid character.

On the side, I enjoy trail running, board gaming, playing piano, reading, climbing, and baking.
