About Me

Hi, I'm Michael. I'm a Colorado native living in Calgary, Canada, and I'm interested in all things related to computer graphics. In addition to working as the Visualization Lead at the Calgary branch of Seequent, I try to keep up on the latest in 3D Graphics through my side projects.

For my MSc, I worked under Michiel van de Panne on physics based character animation, creating a simple scripting language to control the motion of a 2D humanoid character.

On the side, I enjoy trail running, playing piano, reading, climbing, and baking.
